Design Brief

We are specialized in online marketing, video production and marketing automation. We are looking for something modern which is a combination between woodmark and letterform. It's important for us to be able to use the letterform as a standalone (for example as favicon on the website). The logo should also look very clean and modern. Probably even a bit techy.

In regards of color, we are open to any recommendations which are a mix of black/white and one popping color. We are thinking of coloring the "X" maybe. Probably even with a gradient instead of one solid color.
