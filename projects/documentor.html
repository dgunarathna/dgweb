Problem

Create a tool that is specific for documentation.

There are a number of different situations in which someone wants to create a photo (or video) documentation of some or all of their possessions. Examples of such circumstances include:

Documenting what you own as part of getting insurance, either generally or when planning to have your possessions moved to another location. In this case the focus may be on establishing the value and condition of the items in case they are lost or damaged.

Documenting a set of possessions you plan to pass on to children or others, either during your lifetime or at the end of your life. In this case, the focus may be on telling the story behind significant objects (this necklace is the first gift your father gave me, this apron was the first piece of clothing I made myself, etc.).

First I thought creating a website that can store data would be a good solution but finally, with the research data I realize creating a mobile app that can store data in a cloud would be a perfect solution because even if users lost their mobile phone they don't lose their data.

And users no need to buy a camera and also when users can create documentation from their phone they can also view and edit via computer too, which allows two users to use the same documentation at the same time.
